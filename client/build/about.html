About

ArtCharter is a powerful web app which uncovers the untapped artistic heritage of Glasgow's thriving streets and buildings. The location of the city's finest street art and murals are brought to the user's fingertips, whilst mapping out an interesting, diverse route through Glasgow's city centre. The app is designed for locals and visitors alike as the city artwork contains multiple hidden gems that even many locals have not yet discovered.
